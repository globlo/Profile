The fundamental objective of this project was to develop a Home Energy Monitoring System aimed at providing users with an enhanced solution for efficiently managing, monitoring, and analyzing household energy consumption. This innovative system harnessed the capabilities of an mbed ESP8266 microcontroller and was complemented by crucial software components such as the Arduino IDE, Node.js, and React. Leveraging the versatility and power efficiency of the mbed OS platform, the project facilitated rapid prototyping and modularity, ensuring seamless connectivity. As a pivotal outcome, the system achieved real-time transmission of data from the ESP8266 microcontroller to the React-based front end, offering users a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for observing and optimizing their energy consumption practices
